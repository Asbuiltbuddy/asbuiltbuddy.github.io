Privacy Policy

ASBUILTBUDDY is an AutoCAD plugin developed for professional steel construction as built survey automation.

Information Collection

ASBUILTBUDDY does not collect, transmit, store, sell, or share any personal user information, AutoCAD drawing data, survey coordinates, or project files during normal software operation.

License Verification

ASBUILTBUDDY may communicate with Autodesk App Store entitlement services solely for the purpose of verifying a valid software subscription or license purchase.

Technical Support

If a user voluntarily contacts ASBUILTBUDDY support by email, any information provided by the user, including optional AutoCAD log files, screenshots, or drawing extracts, will be used solely for product support and troubleshooting.

Data Sharing

No user data is sold, rented, or provided to third parties.

Contact

For privacy related queries contact: admin@asbuiltbuddy.com
